Blondie

Blondie is a half-whippet/half-lab who was rescued by the Humane Society of Cowlitz County, WA. She was ultra-thin after being dumped by her previous family and became untrusting of any humans. After getting some much needed medical attention, Blondie was up for adoption and selected by the McCarty family who showed her what being a family-member should feel like. Blondie is a very healthy 60 pounds and although takes a while to trust people she is a dog who loves to run after her ball, layout in the sun, and eat slices of apple.
